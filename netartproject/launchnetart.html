Netart Project

My Artist Statement

For this project, I was tasked with creating a piece of Net art, or art that uses the Internet as a medium.,
The project began with a jumble of words from three news articles.

After I received my randomized prompt, "King Car and Cop Dinosaur Was on Date," I had ideas right away.
I decided that the first page would be King Car asking Cop Dinosaur out on the date. Then, I wanted you to be
able to click on either of them to see them getting ready for the date. Then, you could see the actual date and it
would have two possible endings. I chose to just go with basic "Good" and "Bad" endings.

Overall, I think this project turned out really interesting and fun. I love the finished product.
