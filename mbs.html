Mind, Body, and Spirit

With this project, I was tasked with making a triptych, or three creations that go together. I was to do these as photo collages with Photoshop.
The pieces represent the concepts: mind, spirit, and body.​My vision with this project was to create pieces that described me, but in ways that
would be interesting to look at. When the project was first assigned, I figured it would be difficult for me to think of what to do, as I tend to do
very little self-introspection. However, I ended up coming up with ideas relatively quickly. For my mind, I find myself lost in daydreams like one
might be lost in a labyrinth, so I used the Greek myth of the Minotaur in my work. For my spirit, I feel a lot of religious guilt and fear, so I thought
of the frightening biblical depictions of angels. For my body, I simply described myself in ways that I enjoy, like with cats and ghosts. ​ Overall, my
three pieces describe me very well as a person. I enjoyed the process of creating them just about as much as I enjoy the final products.​

This is my documentation of the process of creating these pieces.
